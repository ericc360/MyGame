Super Candy Land Plane Scenario

Air Force Sweet, Ace Pilot Rank #1. How well do you fare in these infinite skies?

Objective: Collect 50 loot in 120 seconds to WIN!

  1. Collect 50 Skies Bounty (Loot)
  2. Manage the Fuel and Health of your Plane
  3. Evade Dastardly Aggressors and Disastrous Dark Clouds

Press R to refuel

If on a tablet or phone, simply rotating the device in order to collect the items. If on a desktop, use the mouse and arrows keys to collect the items.
